Our Ministries & Departments

Serving God through diverse callings, united in one fellowship.

PENSA UMaT ministries are the heartbeat of our fellowship. They provide avenues for students to discover their gifts, serve the Lord, and impact campus with the gospel.

Our Ministries & Departments

Prayer Ministry

Leading intercession, prayer retreats, and providing spiritual covering for the fellowship and campus community.

Evangelism & Outreach

Leading campus evangelism, organizing missions, and reaching out to the lost with the message of Christ.

Music Ministry

Leading worship, training singers and instrumentalists, and creating an atmosphere of praise and worship.

Bible Study & Teaching

Leading in-depth Bible studies, discipleship programs, and teaching God's Word with clarity and relevance.

Ushering / Protocol

Maintaining order during services, welcoming new members, and ensuring a smooth flow of all programs.

Drama / Creative Arts

Using drama, poetry, and other creative expressions to communicate the gospel and edify the church.

Media & Tech Team

Managing sound, livestream, photography, and social media to enhance our worship experience and outreach.

Welfare & Hospitality

Caring for members, new converts, and providing support through various services and outreach programs.
