About PENSA

Pentecost Students and Associates (PENSA) is the student and associate wing of The Church of Pentecost.

Our Vision

To be a dynamic and vibrant Christian fellowship that transforms lives through the power of the Gospel, nurturing students into responsible Christian leaders.

Our Mission

To win students for Christ, nurture them in the Christian faith, and equip them for effective service in God's kingdom through sound biblical teaching and discipleship.

Our Impact

With over 40 years of campus ministry, PENSA has produced numerous Christian leaders making significant impacts in various sectors of society.
