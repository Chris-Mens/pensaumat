Media Department

The Media Department plays a crucial role in shaping the narrative and visual identity of PENSA-UMaT. It consists of several specialized units, each contributing unique skills and expertise to create compelling content across various platforms. By leveraging various forms of media, the department ensures that the church's vision and mission reach as many people as possible. The primary units within the media department include:

Editorial Unit

This unit focuses on content creation, including writing, editing, and curating material for various platforms. It ensures that all media produced aligns with the department's standards and objectives.

 

Graphic Designing Unit

Responsible for visual communication, this unit creates graphics, layouts for our church services and special programs.

 

Live Streaming and Projection Unit

This unit manages live broadcasts and projections for events. It involves technical aspects such as camera operation, sound management, and real-time editing to deliver high-quality live content.

 

Photography Unit

This unit captures high-quality images that document events such as Sunday Church service and special events. The unit also collaborates with the editorial and graphic design units to provide visuals that support written content.

 

Website Unit

This unit focuses on posting contents on our website regulary.

 

OUR VISION

To drive a new wave of evangelism and life transformation on campuses and in communities through digital channels (live streaming and social media posts).

 

MISSION

To engage all members of the church (PENSA-UMaT) and the Campus community with enriching, educational, and motivational content via live streams and social media updates..
