  • ABOUT US

PENSA UMaT, the Pentecost Students and Associates of the University of Mines and Technology, is a dynamic Christian organization under the Church of Pentecost youth ministry. Located on the University of Mines and Technology campus, PENSA UMaT serves as a spiritual and developmental hub for students, welcoming members from the Church of Pentecost and other Bible-believing denominations.

 

The organization focuses on empowering students to be transformative leaders in all areas of life, grounded in faith, academic excellence, leadership development, personal growth, and innovation. Its activities align with the Church's "Possessing the Nations Agenda" through evangelism, leadership training, and outreach.

 

PENSA UMaT comprises seven departments (Evangelism, Bible Study, Prayer, Media, Organizing, Music and Drama, and Transport), three wings (Business and Political Chamber, Ladies Wing, Gents Wing), and several halls and branches, including Akuafo, UMaT, and Mensah Sarbah halls, and off-campus branches for non-residents.

 

Weekly programs, including services, midweek meetings, and departmental gatherings, support members' spiritual, academic, and personal development. PENSA UMaT fosters inclusivity, mentorship, and unity, equipping students to succeed academically, grow spiritually, and positively impact society.

  • Our Vision

    Our vision is to become a formidable and spiritual academic student front that is holistically relevant in the campus setting and beyond, empowering students to excel in their intellectual pursuits, spiritual growth, and personal development.

  • Our Mission

    Our mission is to build and empower members who are spiritually sound, academically strong, and socially relevant to transform their world for Jesus. We will foster a deepening spiritual life, rooted in faith, prayer, and scripture while pursuing academic excellence, innovation, and critical thinking. Through an all-inclusive and engaging framework, we will cultivate social awareness, empathy, and responsible citizenship, developing inclusive leaders who serve and transform their communities. We will engage in holistic development, mentorship, and lifelong learning, equipping members to be agents of positive change and social impact. By building bridges between faith, academia, and society, we will inspire a culture of collaboration, creativity, and innovation, embodying God's love and grace in all we do.

  • Our Values

    Our values gear towards a commitment to fostering a thriving spiritual community, supporting academic achievement, nurturing leadership potential through the business and polical chamber, engaging in meaningful outreach, empowering memebers for transformative impact, promoting continuous personal growth, and encouraging innovative thinking. This is who we are!
