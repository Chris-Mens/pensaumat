Who We Are

PENSA (PENtecost Students and Associates) UMaT is a vibrant Christian student body under the Church of Pentecost, operating within the University of Mines and Technology (UMaT) community. As part of the larger PENSA Ghana network, we are committed to nurturing students spiritually, academically, and socially.

We are more than just a fellowship - we are a family that supports, encourages, and grows together in Christ. Our doors are open to students from all backgrounds who seek to know God and make Him known on campus and beyond.

Our Vision

To be a dynamic and vibrant Christian fellowship that transforms lives through the power of the Gospel, nurturing students into responsible Christian leaders who will impact their communities and the world for Christ.

Our Mission

  • To win students for Christ through evangelism and discipleship
  • To nurture spiritual growth through sound biblical teaching
  • To develop Christian leaders for the church and society
  • To foster a loving and supportive Christian community

Our History & Legacy

Celebrating our journey of faith and impact

1980

Establishment

PENSA UMaT was officially established as a campus ministry, starting with a small group of dedicated students.

2010

Growth & Expansion

The fellowship experienced significant growth, leading to the establishment of various ministries and departments.

2015

New Meeting Venue

Acquired a dedicated meeting space on campus, allowing for more comprehensive ministry activities.

2020

Digital Ministry

Successfully transitioned to online ministry during the global pandemic, reaching more students than ever before.

Present

Continuing the Legacy

Today, PENSA UMaT continues to be a beacon of light on campus, with hundreds of students actively involved in various ministries and outreach programs.
