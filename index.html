      Who Are We?

      PENSA UMaT is more than just a campus ministry. We are a close-knit community of believers dedicated to spiritual growth, academic success, and leadership development. As part of the Church of Pentecost's youth ministry, we welcome students from all Christian backgrounds who are looking for a supportive community during their time at university.

      Who We Are?

      PENSA UMaT is more than just a campus ministry - we are a close-knit community of believers dedicated to spiritual growth, academic success, and leadership development. As part of the Church of Pentecost's youth ministry, we welcome students from all Christian backgrounds who are looking for a supportive community during their time at university.

        PENSA Wings

        • Ladies' and Gents' Wing

          The Ladies' and Gents' Wing is a vital part of PENSA UMaT, representing the Women's and PEMEN (Pentecost Men's Ministry) of the Church of Pentecost. This wing focuses on the spiritual growth and development of both male and female students.

          Read more

        • Business and Political Chamber

          The Business and Political Chamber is an initiative of the Church of Pentecost designed to integrate faith-based principles with the practical realms of business and politics.

          Read more

        • PENSA Professional Guild

          The PENSA Professional Guild (PeSPG) is a key initiative supporting the Youth Ministry's 2023-2027 vision: 'Grounded in Christ, Reaching Everyone and Taking over Everywhere for Jesus.' This program helps integrate faith with professional development for our members.
